Baron is the founder and CCO of Baron & Baron, Inc.,
a boutique advertising agency specializing in fashion,
fragrance and cosmetics luxury brands.

He has "shaped the visual identities" of global brands like
Calvin Klein, Givenchy, Fendi, and Michael Kors
by creating the right mix of television, digital and print campaigns,
product and packaging design.
